# AI Hackathon // Build. Deploy. Ship.

Welcome, operator. This is your mission control for the company AI Hackathon — open to
**everyone**: finance, HR, operations, and engineering. No cloud expertise required.

## How it works

1. **Sign in** with your hackathon account.
2. **Grab the deploy Skill** and hand it (plus your API token) to your AI coding agent.
3. **Ask your agent to build and deploy** your app, API, or static site.
4. Your app goes live on a shared **Azure Container Apps Express** environment — you get a
   public URL instantly. No Azure credentials, ever.
5. **Iterate freely.** Every version is snapshotted; roll back anytime.

> Tip: You don't write any deployment YAML. Your agent uses the platform API for you.

## The rules

- One environment, many teams — be kind to shared compute.
- Pick the smallest replica size that runs your app (`1cpu2ram`, `2cpu4ram`, `4cpu8ram`).
- Keep secrets out of your images.
- Have fun and demo something bold. ⚡
